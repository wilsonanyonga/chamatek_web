WELCOME TO CHAMATEK

Chamatek is a leading software to help manage chama operations by providing peer to peer lending platform to help chamas and table banking members keep records and manage their funds.


Why Chamatek

Chama-Centric Focus:
Chamatek is tailor-made for your chama's unique needs, emphasizing the strength of collective financial power. Our platform is designed to foster trust and collaboration among chama members.

Transparent and Fair:
At Chamatek, transparency is our core value. We ensure fair and equitable lending practices within your chama, with clearly defined terms and conditions. Your financial well-being is our top priority.

User-Friendly Platform:
Chamatek offers an intuitive and user-friendly interface, making it easy for chama members to navigate and participate in peer-to-peer lending. Whether you're a tech expert or a novice, Chamatek is designed with you in mind.

How It Works

Chama Registration:
Download the Chamatek app and register your chama. Invite members to join and start leveraging the collective financial strength of your group.

Loan Requests:
Members can request loans directly within the app. Chamatek ensures a quick and straightforward loan application process.

Peer-to-Peer Lending:
Chama members review loan requests and contribute to funding. Experience the simplicity of borrowing and lending directly within your trusted chama network.

Repayment and Credit Building:
Timely repayments contribute to positive credit scores within your chama. Build a strong financial profile and access better financial opportunities.
