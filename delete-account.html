Delete Account

Dear Chamatek Member,
We value your privacy and are committed to ensuring that your personal data is handled securely and responsibly. If you wish to have your Chamatek account details deleted from our system, please follow the instructions below:
Compose an Email: Write an email to info@chamatek.com requesting the deletion of your account details.
Subject Line: Please use the subject line: "Request for Deletion of Chamatek Account Details".
Content of the Email: In the body of the email, provide the following details:
Your full name.
The email address associated with your Chamatek account.
Any other relevant information that can help us identify your account.
Confirmation of Deletion: Once we receive your request, we will confirm the deletion of your account details via email. Please allow us some time to process your request.
Final Note: Please note that deleting your account details will result in the permanent removal of your personal data from our system. This action cannot be reversed, and you will no longer have access to your Chamatek account.
If you have any questions or concerns regarding this process, please do not hesitate to reach out to us at info@chamatek.com. We are here to assist you.
Thank you for being a part of Chamatek.
