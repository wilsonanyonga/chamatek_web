WHAT WE DO

Chamatek is filled with alot of features such as:

Group Loans

Chamatek facilitates easy and secure group loans within your chama. Say goodbye to lengthy processes and high-interest rates. Borrow and lend directly within your trusted group.

Flexible Repayment Options

Customize your repayment plans to suit the unique needs of your chama. Chamatek allows you to define flexible terms that work for everyone, ensuring a stress-free lending experience.

Credit Scoring System

Build a credit history within your chama. Chamatek rewards responsible financial behavior, making it easier for members to access larger loans and better interest rates over time.

Savings and Investments

Chamatek isn't just about borrowing; it's about growing together. Utilize our platform to pool savings and explore investment opportunities that align with your chama's financial goals.
